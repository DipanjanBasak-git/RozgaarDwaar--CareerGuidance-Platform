Government Employment Schemes

Explore various government programs designed to provide employment opportunities, skill development, and financial support to Indian youth.

Available Schemes

PM Kaushal Vikas Yojana

Skill development program providing industry-relevant training and certification to youth across India.

Target: 10 Million Youth
Stipend: ₹8,000/month
Duration: 3-6 months

Rozgar Mela

Direct employment opportunities through government job fairs and recruitment drives.

Target: 1 Million Jobs
Salary: ₹15,000-50,000
Duration: Permanent

Apprenticeship Training

On-the-job training with leading companies to gain practical work experience.

Target: 5 Million Youth
Stipend: ₹5,000-15,000
Duration: 1-3 years

Startup India

Support for young entrepreneurs to start their own businesses with funding and mentorship.

Target: 1 Million Startups
Funding: Up to ₹1 Crore
Duration: 3-5 years

Women Empowerment

Special schemes for women including skill development, employment, and entrepreneurship support.

Target: 2 Million Women
Support: ₹10,000-25,000
Duration: 6-12 months

Rural Employment

Employment opportunities in rural areas through various government initiatives and programs.

Target: 5 Million Rural Youth
Wage: ₹200-400/day
Duration: 100 days/year

General Eligibility Criteria

Age Requirements

  • Minimum: 18 years
  • Maximum: 35 years (varies by scheme)
  • Relaxation for reserved categories

Educational Qualification

  • 10th Standard (minimum)
  • 12th Standard (preferred)
  • Graduate/Diploma (for some schemes)

Document Requirements

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Educational Certificates
  • Income Certificate
  • Bank Account Details

Family Income

  • Annual income below ₹8 lakhs
  • No family member in government job
  • Priority for BPL families

How to Apply

1

Registration

Create your account on RozgaarDwaar portal with basic details and documents.

2

Profile Completion

Complete your profile with educational qualifications, skills, and preferences.

3

Scheme Selection

Browse and select the schemes that match your eligibility and interests.

4

Application Submission

Submit your application with required documents and wait for verification.

5

Selection & Training

Get selected and start your training or employment as per the scheme.
